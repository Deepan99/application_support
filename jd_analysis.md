## 2. Key Themes to Highlight

During the interview, BNP Paribas will evaluate you not just on technical skills, but on how you operate under pressure in a financial environment. You should weave these three themes into your answers:

### Theme A: Minimizing MTTR (Mean Time to Resolution)
* **What it means**: When a client-facing application is down, every minute costs money and damages reputation.
* **Your angle**: Emphasize how you use **KEDB** and **Linux troubleshooting commands** to quickly isolate whether an issue is resource-related (e.g., disk full, CPU maxed, memory leak), application-related (hung process), or configuration-related. 

### Theme B: Proactive Support over Reactive Support
* **What it means**: The business prefers support analysts who prevent issues, not just fix them after they break.
* **Your angle**: Discuss how you used **monitoring alerts** and **Ansible playbooks** to automate daily checks and resolve minor issues before they escalated to client-facing incidents.

### Theme C: Standardized & Clear Communication
* **What it means**: During a major incident, multiple business stakeholders and technical teams need updates.
* **Your angle**: Highlight your ability to translate complex technical issues into clear, business-friendly status updates. Focus on your collaboration skills across different teams.
